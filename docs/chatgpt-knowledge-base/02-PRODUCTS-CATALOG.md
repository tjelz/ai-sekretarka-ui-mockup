## ü§ñ 2. AI Receptionist

### Product Description

24/7 automated phone answering service powered by ElevenLabs AI technology, speaking natural Polish language to handle customer calls, book appointments, and never miss an opportunity.

### How It Works

1. **Call Routing:** Customer calls your business number
2. **AI Greeting:** Natural-sounding AI receptionist answers in Polish
3. **Conversation:** AI handles inquiries, provides information, books appointments
4. **Action:** Transcripts and bookings sent to you via email/SMS
5. **Human Handoff:** Complex queries transferred to human staff

### Technology

- **Voice Engine:** ElevenLabs neural voice synthesis
- **Natural Language:** Advanced Polish language understanding
- **Learning System:** Improves with every conversation
- **Integration:** Works with your existing phone system
