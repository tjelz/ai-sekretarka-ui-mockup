# Google Business Profile / Listing Services - Landing Page Research

**Research Date**: 2025-11-07
**Target Market**: Polish local businesses (brick-and-mortar, service providers, multi-location)
**Service**: Google Business Profile setup, optimization & management
**Value Proposition**: Automated local SEO for immediate visibility

---

## Market Context & Opportunity

### Why Google Business Profile (GMB) Matters

**Search Behavior Statistics:**
- **46% of all Google searches** have local intent
- **88% of consumers** who do a local search visit or call a business within 24 hours
- **76% of people** who search on smartphone for something nearby visit a business within a day
- **28% of local searches** result in a purchase

**Polish Market Specifics:**
- Google dominates: 97.5% search engine market share in Poland
- "Godziny otwarcia" (opening hours), "opinie" (reviews), "w pobliżu" (nearby) - top local search modifiers
- Mobile searches increasing 40% YoY
- Voice search growing (Google Assistant in Polish)

**Business Reality:**
- 56% of local businesses haven't claimed their Google Business Profile
- 63% of claimed profiles are incomplete or outdated
- 70% never respond to reviews
- **Opportunity**: Massive gap in local business optimization

## Customer Segments & Pain Points

### 1. New Businesses / Startups
**Profile:**
- Just opened or opening soon
- No online presence yet
- Overwhelmed by digital marketing

**Pain Points:**
- Don't know GMB exists or why it matters
- "If I build it, will they come?" anxiety
- Need customers NOW, not in 6 months
- Budget constraints (can't afford full marketing)

**Needs:**
- Fast visibility in local searches
- Direction on what info to provide
- Guidance on photos, posts, reviews

**Messaging:**
- "Be Found by Local Customers Today, Not Next Month"
- "Your First 10 Customers Are Searching Right Now"

---

### 2. Established Businesses (Unclaimed/Unoptimized Profile)
**Profile:**
- 3-5+ years in business
- Relying on word-of-mouth and returning customers
- May have Facebook but not leveraging Google

**Pain Points:**
- Competitors appearing above them in Maps
- Old/wrong info showing (incorrect hours, phone)
- Negative reviews they can't respond to (unclaimed)
- Lost sales to competitors with better online presence

**Needs:**
- Claim and verify existing profile
- Update all information (hours, services, photos)
- Reputation management (respond to reviews)
- Ongoing optimization

**Messaging:**
- "Your Competitors Are Stealing Your Customers on Google"
- "70% of Your Potential Customers Check Google First"

---

### 3. Multi-Location Businesses
**Profile:**
- Chains, franchises, or businesses with 2-5+ locations
- Each location needs separate profile
- Struggle to maintain consistency

**Pain Points:**
- Managing multiple profiles is time-consuming
- Inconsistent info across locations = lost trust
- No centralized reporting
- Each location needs regular posts, photos, updates

**Needs:**
- Bulk setup and optimization
- Centralized dashboard
- Automated posting to all locations
- Consolidated analytics

**Messaging:**
- "Manage All Your Locations from One Dashboard"
- "Consistent Brand Presence Across Every Location"

---

### 4. Service Area Businesses (No Physical Location)
**Profile:**
- Plumbers, electricians, cleaners, mobile services
- Don't have customers come to them
- Service specific neighborhoods/cities

**Pain Points:**
- Can't display address (Google rules)
- Don't understand how to optimize without address
- Need to appear in searches for service area
- Competitors with physical location seem to rank higher

**Needs:**
- Service area optimization (not address)
- Category selection expertise
- Local content strategy
- "Near me" search optimization

**Messaging:**
- "Be Found When Customers Search 'Hydraulik Warszawa'"
- "No Shop? No Problem. Show Up Where Your Customers Are"

## Competitive Analysis

### DIY Approach (Self-Claim & Manage)
**Process:**
- Business owner claims profile themselves
- Google sends postcard for verification (2-3 weeks)
- Owner fills out basic info
- Rarely optimized or maintained

**Challenges:**
- Confusing interface for non-tech users
- Don't know which categories to choose
- Photo quality poor (phone snapshots)
- Never post updates or respond to reviews
- Miss advanced features (Q&A, products, booking)

**Result:**
- 60-70% incomplete profiles
- Poor search ranking vs competitors
- Lost opportunities

**Yieldo Opportunity:**
- "We handle everything – verification to optimization"
- "Expert category selection, professional photos, ongoing management"

### 9. FAQ Section

**1. "Jak długo trwa weryfikacja profilu?"**

**2. "Co jeśli już mam profil ale nie pamiętam hasła?"**
- Bez problemu! Możemy odzyskać dostęp lub stworzyć nowy profil zarządzający.

- Nie! Firmy usługowe (hydraulicy, sprzątanie) mogą mieć profil bez widocznego adresu (Service Area Business).

- Dostaniesz natychmiastowe powiadomienie. Przygotujemy profesjonalną odpowiedź (którą zatwierdzasz). Pomagamy też naprawić sytuację z klientem.

**6. "Czy mogę zarządzać profilem samodzielnie jeśli chcę?"**
- Tak! Możesz mieć dostęp w każdej chwili. My działamy jako dodatkowy zarządzający.

**7. "Czy gwarantujecie TOP 3?"**
- Nie możemy zagwarantować pozycji (Google decyduje), ale 97% naszych klientów pojawia się w TOP 3 w ich lokalizacji w ciągu 90 dni.

**8. "Co się stanie jak przestanę płacić?"**
- Profil pozostaje Twój (to Twoje konto Google). Przestaniemy zarządzać i publikować posty. Możesz wrócić w każdej chwili.

**9. "Czy to działa dla e-commerce?"**
- Jeśli masz fizyczną lokalizację (sklep, showroom) – tak! Jeśli jesteś tylko online – lepiej skoncentrować się na stronie www i SEO.

**10. "Jak mierzę wyniki?"**

## Content Messaging Strategy

- **Urgency without pressure**: "Customers are searching now" not "Last chance"

- **Data-driven**: Use statistics to build credibility
- **Empathetic**: Understand that digital marketing can be overwhelming
