**3. Pricing Not Competitive**
- **Risk**: Market rejects pricing, too expensive or too cheap
- **Mitigation**: A/B test pricing tiers, offer limited-time launch discount to gauge demand
- **Timeline**: Monitor first 50 leads, adjust by Week 6 if needed

**4. Conversion Rate Low**
- **Risk**: Traffic but no leads (<10% conversion)
- **Mitigation**: Aggressive A/B testing, user recordings (Hotjar), exit surveys
- **Timeline**: Implement tracking Week 1, analyze after 500 visitors

**5. Cross-Sell Confusion**
- **Risk**: Too many products = choice paralysis
- **Mitigation**: Clear navigation, quiz/configurator ("Which service do you need?"), package recommendations
- **Timeline**: Launch configurator tool Month 2
